Exploded View of A Bluetooth Mouse

 Exploded view vector drawing of the Logitech bluetooth mouse with different parts, 2018

 Another vector drawing I create using Illustrator. This time I challenged myself by creating a projection of parts of a mouse and combined it into an exploded view sheet. This is an important step in product design. I had drawn other technical product sheet (perspective, assembly and engineering drawings) before, but have never try exploded view, which is the reason I made this drawing.

 Drawing the exploded view was a good experience for me. I had to consider turning a 3D object into a drawing that can show how it is assembled. Choosing the angle and the composition is one of the thing I learnt from this project.
