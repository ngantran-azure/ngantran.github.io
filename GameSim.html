clOWN - GAME SIMULATION VIDEO

VIDEO EDITING

Adobe After Effects and Procreate, 2023


Description

Game simulation video of a scene from my original game Tales of The Foolish Justice made on Twine.

Process

Player walk sequence

All the assets were drawn on Procreate, including the frame-by-frame walk sequence. After importing all the assets into After Effects, I edited the elements so it looks like an actual game playing session. In addition, I tried to recreate the effects of when player is scrolling through the options of the game.
