Embarrassment


Procreate, 2020

The first of the trio Emotions.

A visualization of my emotions after an embarrassing incident. I condensed my embarrassment into 3 different emojis: The Skull, The Clown and The Crying. Combining all three is my Embarrassment.

Anxiety


Procreate, 2021

The second of the trio Emotions.

Similar to Embarrassment, Anxiety was created using the same method of combining emojis that represent the emotion.I tried to convey it in a badly written poem: "When time stops, when one feels like their soul dried out, when their eyes are clouded by the thoughts, is when Anziety comes around"

Protection

Procreate, 2021

The third of the trio Emotions.

A contortionist trying to protect their identity with all their might. This is a visualization of my anxiety and the fear of being in the community. Not only it visualized my anxiety, the hidden eye mark on the contortionist's body also signify the hope to be seen with what I've got.

Unicorn: Reality
