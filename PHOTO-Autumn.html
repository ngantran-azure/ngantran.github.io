DMA

Autumn,2020

 Photo taken by using DLSR and enhanced through Lightroom

 For me, Fall is my favorite season of the year. I like the chilly winds but it's still warm enough to not wear a thick coat. I like it better than Spring because while Spring is pretty, it still has the winter cold winds. I also like Summer, but California's summer is dry enough to like it less. It's Fall that is warm and cool enough to make me happy.

 I didn't retouch much on alll the photos I took. My idea in editing photos is that I try to reserve the natural aspect of the picture as much as I could. For Autumn, I cropped the raw footage and enhanced the red in the leaves, while blurring the car in the back
