Cotton Candy, 2020

 Photo taken by using a DSLR camera and enhanced through Lightroom

 For a long time, I've always been interestd in the sky. The sky is an amazing thing that people tend to take it for granted. Especially after 2020's California wildfire turned the sky into a bright red, blue sky seems like a luxury. That is why after the fire I like to appreciate the pretty blue sky more.

  I took Cotton Candy while walking back home. The mixture of the blue clouds and pinkish sunset remind me of the cotton cnady I used to eat as a kid. The raw photo was a bit dull, so I used Lightroom to increase the intensity of the blue sky. I also inceased the brightness of the photo by a notch
