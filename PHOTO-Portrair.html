Self-portrait, 2020

 A photograph I took while experiment with the manual setting of a DSLR camera. It was pretty last ata night when I took this photo. I was trying to familiarize myself with the camera. the saw my refelction on the window class and had to capture the moment. I played with the light setting, the aperture and the shutterspeed.

 The reason I choose this photo as a part of my portfolio is it describe me as a person. For a long time, I'm standing in between the lines of my identity. I know what I want to do in life, but sometimes I actually don't know anything. Everything is blurry to me sometimes. I can look straight into my inner self, but will I be a better person, or my future self would be disastrous? I looked straight into my reflection, but I only saw the unstable self.
