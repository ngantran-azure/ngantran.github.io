Ronias - An Unknown World


Description: You are a dinosaur facing the end of your species during the Jurassic era. You can choose to run and avoid being hit by meteor and lava, or you can choose to fight against the alien invader and save the planet.

The game have 2 modes: runner and shooter. Jurassic Escape: Runner is a visual enhanced version of the infamous Google dinosaur game. You as the last dinosaur alive have to run to avoid meteors and lava puddles in order to stay alive. This game mode have no ending, so you can challenge your friends for the highest score.

For Jurassic Escape: Shooter, the player is equipped with a tesla coil on their head that can shoot lighting bolts to destroy the enemy and defeat the alien boss. You have to defear 12 aliens to unlock the key to the boss' level.
