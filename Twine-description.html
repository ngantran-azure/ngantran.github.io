Tales of the Foolish Justice (Twine version)

A short visual novel game about the player met a young clownette and their short interaction

CLICK HERE TO PLAY

Description: I have always wanted to share people the stories of the characters I made. For this story, I wanted to show a character that is supposed to make other laugh but won't let others make fun of her. Beside most of the background images and gifs are royalty free images, the story and characters are all mine.

The story is you are working for a classified mail delivery service, and you are on your 2nd day of the trip. You stop by a town to rest for the night when based on your choice, you would meet a young clownette. Depends on the choices you made, you either stay alive or you'll be killed. So make good choices!
